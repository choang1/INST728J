Hello! My name is Cynthia Hoang,

but I prefer to go by Cindy. It's much more familiar.

I have quite a range of hobbies, but to break things down I like to spend my free time

  • trying out new things,
  • improving myself creatively through design and illustration,
  • photographing spaces,
  • and relaxing at home with my loved ones.
When it comes to food, I'm certainly not one to be picky.
As a Vietnamese-American I am accustomed to quite a few odd dishes here and there and embrace them wholeheartedly.

Some of my favorite Vietnamese dishes:

  1. Bo Bia
  2. Banh Xeo
  3. Chung Vit Lon
  4. Banh It Ram
  5. Pho

Why should I fear how odd a dish may be if others have enjoyed it? I see food as a window to culture and am open to trying anything--which makes me the perfect food and travel buddy I'd say.

I consider myself a sentimental creature and seek to capture emotions and shared experiences in photography, art, and design.
Expressing myself creatively has allowed me to slow down and appreciate the people in my life and the simplest joys in a moment.
I was once advised by a painting professor to live for the experiences, the memories, and not solely for the money.
He's right, life's too short to be focused on materialistic means. A life is more fulfilling when you can take the time to appreciate your day-to-day.
